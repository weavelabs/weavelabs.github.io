Learn the basics of Web Programming

Most corporates and industry experts have acknowledged the gap between theortical knowledge imparted in universities and the real life scenarios encountered in software development companies. Web Development Basics Bootcamp tries to reduce this gap by providing training for entry-level aspirants who is beginning their life as a software engineer.

A lot of fresh graduates joining the software industry is of opinion that they wished they knew more when joining their first company. Getting to know the terminologies and basic technologies before-hand, helps a fresh graduate breeze through the entry level training offered in these companies.

This Bootcamp gives a clear idea on the tools and technologies used by web developers.
